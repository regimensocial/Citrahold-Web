what is Citrahold?

Simply put, Citrahold provides you with everything you need to easily move game saves from a physical 3DS to a 3DS emulator on your computer (and vice versa).

This sounds like a niche issue, but this solves a major issue for me. I quite enjoy emulating Pokemon at high resolutions, but I still want to play it on the train.

I would like to keep my saves though, and be able to move them back and forth. Kind of like a poor man's Switch (as someone else has put it).

And thus, Citrahold was created (Citra being the name of the main emulator, -hold because you physically hold your saves within the 3DS).

A save manager (Checkpoint) is still required, but this massively speeds things up.

Citrahold exists as three:

  • Citrahold 3DS (a 3DS Homebrew application, available as a .cia or .3dsx)
  • Citrahold PC (an executable program, for Windows, Mac, and Linux)
  • Citrahold Web (this very platform)

And also Citrahold Server... But if you're here, you likely don't need to worry about that.
