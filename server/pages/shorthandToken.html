shorthand token generation.

you can generate a shorthand token here! You need one of these to link your 3DS (and PC) to this account.
If you need another token, simply press the generate button above again!
  • Enter it on Citrahold 3DS by going into
    Settings -> "Input a shorthand token"
  • Or enter it on Citrahold PC in the box on startup, and then click Verify

You will need to generate a new shorthand token every time you link a device (PC or 3DS).

It must be entered exactly correctly, case included.
